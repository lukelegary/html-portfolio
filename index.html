The Best Movies According to Di Allegaris

My top 3 five movies of all-time

It's A Wonderful Life

Incredible vision of practilly a whole life of a man, who was very happy, had many virtues. However he was blind by the resentment of not fulfiling his imagined happy life.

Coach Carter

A man who built a successful life, paved from his basketball prowess and academic performance, tries to teach those lessons to many teenagers in a troubled neighbourhood who have what is necessary, except for studying, to make their own way into life

October Sky

In a 1950s small coal-mining city, a teenager finds his way out of Coalwood, with the help of his friends and community, building an amateur rocket project. His father however, does not see with the same eyes his passion. One look to the sky, the other looks to the ground.
